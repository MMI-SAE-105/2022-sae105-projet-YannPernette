The music which marked the year 2014

Infinite Power

Beginnings of the TheFatRat

Infinite Power is a music released in 2014 composed by TheFatRat. Christian Büttner from his real name is a DJ and producer of German electro music. He is considered a musical genius of the year 2010 and has released many titles. He began his career composing music for advertising, television and radio.

Then, in 2011, he will remix for electro artists. He will work with Avicii and Martin Solveig. He will release under his nickname TheFatRat his first music soon after, always on the theme of electro. Some are well known on the Internet today like Xenogenesis or Time Lapse.

“Its lively rhythm and basses have made the music a worldwide success on the Internet.”

Career and success

But in 2014, he became extremely popular by releasing free-usage music on Youtube. Indeed, it allows other creators to use its music for free. These songs will also be added to the video game «Geometry Dash». This will allow fans to integrate TheFatRat’s music directly into the levels they have created. TheFatRat is then gaining notoriety and will become one of the most popular music creators on the Internet with more than 6 million subscribers on YouTube.

Infinite Power is a music that marked 2014. It takes all the codes of modern electro while keeping the trademark of TheFatRat. Its lively rhythm and basses have made the music a worldwide success on the Internet.

Nostalgia of an era

This music reminds me of a very good time in my life: my entry in middle school. During those years everything was easier. I didn’t have to worry about making dinner in the evening. I didn’t worry about shopping. And I didn’t care if I had enough money to finish the month.

It was what you might call the perfect life. Today all has changed and I have acquired responsibilities. I do not regret that time though. I have simply evolved.
