About me

Who am I ?

- 18 years old

- Student in first year of the BUT Métiers du Multimédia et de l’Internet “MMI” in Montbéliard

- Owner of the General Bachelor’s degree

Project goal

The goal of this project was to present to my future self (10 years later) six elements. These elements constitute the articles of the website. So I will always have a memory of 2022. I could look at the site in the future and remember that time.

My approach

I wanted to show the evolution of the world through this project. Those who visit this website must understand that the world is constantly changing. The articles are presented in chronological order on the homepage to facilitate the understanding of this evolution.
